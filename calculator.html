About Zakat Calculation

Zakat is obligatory charity due on wealth that has been in your possession for one complete lunar (Hijri) year.

Nisab: The minimum amount of wealth required before Zakat becomes obligatory. This calculator shows both Gold and Silver nisab values based on current market prices.

Gold vs Silver Nisab: Many scholars recommend using the silver nisab as it allows more people to fulfill this obligation, while others prefer gold nisab. Consult your local scholar for guidance.

Jewelry: In the Hanafi school, personal use jewelry worn by women is exempt from Zakat. Toggle this option if applicable.
